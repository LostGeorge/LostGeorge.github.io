What I've Done:
SmarterCache
SmarterCache is an exploration of optimal cache replacement through machine learning. Working with Dr. Ymir Vigfusson of Emory and PHD candidate Jason Yang of Carnegie Mellon, I developed a shallow deep learning framework using PyTorch that outperformed modern-day cache replacement heuristics. Smartercache functions in a generalizable domain; the MLP framework integrates time series analysis for tailored cache state representations.
Farm-a-bowl
An e-commerce website, Farmabowl was built to present a potential business engaged in delivering fresh-food bowls directly to your home. Built using vanilla Javascript, JQuery, and HTML/CSS with Seong Ryoo of Georgia Tech and Steven Yao of UT Austin. Won first place state at Georgia Future Business Leaders of America 2018.
Diagnosis Clarity
Connecting patients with psychiatrists and psychologists, Diagnosis Clarity is a mood-tracking web application that allows patients and their doctors to view how their mood changes over time with daily context and through visualization. Designed using a Flask and SQLAlchemy backend and HTML/CSS frontend with Nicholas Lim and Rahul Shahi of Brown University.
Arcadian Dreams
Arcadian Dreams is a modern synthesis of classic arcade games during its "golden age." Developed using GameMaker, it brings the player through various stages inspired by Space Invaders, Joust, or Q-Bert, with challenging AI enemies. Created with Seong Ryoo and Steven Yao. Won first place nationals at National Future Business Leaders of America 2017.
